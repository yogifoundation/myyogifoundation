Who We Are

Our History

 The Yogi Foundation was founded in 2024 by a group of compassionate individuals who recognized the need for an organization dedicated to promoting well-being and community support. Since then, it has grown into a thriving nonprofit, organizing blood donation camps, promoting yoga and meditation, and supporting various initiatives to uplift society.

Guru Gorakhnath Temple

 The Yogi Foundation is dedicated to building Guru Gorakhnath temples, creating sacred spaces for spiritual growth, devotion, and community connection. Our mission is to honor tradition and provide a place for reflection and worship.
